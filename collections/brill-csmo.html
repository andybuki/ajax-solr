The Chinese Students’ Monthly

CONTENT: Published between 1906 to 1931 - an important turning point in Chinese history - the magazine is the first Chinese students’ magazine published in the United States and evolved into the official organ of the enlarged Chinese student organization. Amongst its contributors are many important and well-known figures such as Hu Shi and Chao Yuanren.
