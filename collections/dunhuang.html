Dunhuang Historical Material 敦煌史料

CONTENT: The collection assembles the holdings of Dunhuang material from the Beijing National Library (北图), the Beijing University Library (北大藏), the Dunhuang Museum and Research Institute (敦博 ， 敦研) and other Chinese libraries such as the Tianjin Art Museum (天津艺术博物馆) as well as materials now in England, France and Russia. The content is (of course!) not complete, but provides a sound corpus of Dunhuang materials concerning religion, economy, law, language, literature, science and technology, art and customs written between the 4th and 11th century.
