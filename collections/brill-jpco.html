CONTENT: Covering the years 1900 to 1941 the Weekly Edition of the Japan Chronicle (or Kobe Chronicle as it was named in 1900-1901) together with the Daily Edition (1929-1949) and the Commercial Supplement (1915-1941) provide news and opinions on Japan and East Asia and builds a lively complement to the North China Herald.
