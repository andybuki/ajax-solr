Qing Imperial Encyclopedia (Gujin tushu jicheng) 古今圖書集成

CONTENT: With 6,117 topical sections on over 800,000 pages, the Gujin tushu jicheng 古今圖書集成 is the largest still extent encyclopedic compilation of Chinese history. Started by Chen Menglei 陳夢雷under the imperial order of the Kangxi emperor 康熙 (r. 1661-1722) between 1701 und 1706 it was published and printed with moveable copper type in 1726 under the supervision of Jiang Tingxi 蔣廷錫.Each section assembles excerpts from a great variety of sources from early writings up to the 17th century.
