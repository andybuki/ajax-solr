Mobilizing East Asia (1931-1954)

CONTENT: The Collection offers access to extremely rare, many times even unique English-language newspapers, magazines and pamphlets published inside Asia, following the descent into war in East and South-East Asia from the turn of the twentieth century to the 1950s. Included are several Manchuria and Japan newspapers and selection of 20 book titles related to the period and area.

NOTE: Links to the issue or book are provided with the search hits; no links to the individual pages in the database are possible.
