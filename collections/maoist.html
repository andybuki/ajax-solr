The Maoist Legacy Database

CONTENT: The Maoist Legacy Database is an open-access archive containing thousands of items on a range of topics related to how the CCP dealt with legacies of the Maoist past. Most items date from the late 1970s and 1980s, although there are a substantial number of documents from previous periods. The database also includes more than 850 caricatures (*manhua*) related to the Gang of Four.

NOTE: The search result includes a link to the Maoist Legacy Database. Users are redirected to the database registration page where prior to accessing the site they are required to agree to Terms and Conditions in order to register a free account. Once logged in, instructions for searching by item number are prominently displayed on the home page.
