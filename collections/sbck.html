Sibu congkan 四部叢刊

CONTENT: The Sibu congkan 四部叢刊 is a collection of 472 (or 504 depending on how to count) photolithography facsimiles of authoritative editions of historical monographs published by the Commercial Press in three series between 1919 and 1936. The titles span Song, Yuan and Ming (and some Qing) dynasty editions and focus on the quality of the original text and its truthful reproduction. Its approach thus differs from the Sibu beiyao (published from 1924 to 1936), which newly typeset and corrected text errors of the original.
