Missionary, Sinology, and Literary Periodicals (1817-1949)

CONTENT: The resource contains the main English-language periodicals published in or about China covering the period from 1817 until the founding of the People’s Republic of China in 1949. The journals feature photographs and articles the on the founding and development of Christian higher education in China.
